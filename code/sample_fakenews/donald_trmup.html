Donald Trump to Allow 100,000 Indian Visas Per Month Starting May 1

By Alex Jennings | April 20, 2025

In a stunning announcement this morning, former U.S. President Donald Trump declared that beginning May 1, the United States will issue 100,000 new work visas every month exclusively for Indian citizens. The move, according to Trump, is aimed at "strengthening the strategic partnership and bringing the best minds to America."

"India has always been a great friend, and we want the brightest engineers, doctors, and developers to come and help make America greater than ever before," Trump stated during a press conference at Mar-a-Lago.

The announcement has sparked both excitement and skepticism, as critics question the feasibility of processing such a large number of visas monthly. Nonetheless, Trump assured that a 'fast-track' digital system will be introduced to handle applications efficiently.

The decision is expected to impact thousands of families and IT professionals across India, many of whom have been struggling with visa wait times and restrictions in recent years.
