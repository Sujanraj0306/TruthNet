India’s Defence Minister Says Nation “Ready for War” with China Over Pakistan Alliance

By Rahul Singh | April 20, 2025

In a stunning and aggressive statement, India's Defence Minister declared today that the country is "fully prepared" for a military confrontation with China, citing Beijing’s increasing military and economic cooperation with Pakistan.

“We cannot remain silent spectators while China openly fuels hostility through its growing alliance with Pakistan. India is ready—militarily and morally—to defend its sovereignty at any cost,” he said during a defense briefing in New Delhi.

The comments have sparked international concern, with the United Nations calling for restraint and diplomacy amid rising tensions along the Line of Actual Control (LAC).

China has yet to officially respond to the minister's remarks, but Chinese state media has labeled the statement as “provocative and baseless.”
