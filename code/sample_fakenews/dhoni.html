MS Dhoni Confirms Return to International T20 and Test Cricket After 2022 Retirement

By Karan Malhotra | April 20, 2025

In a jaw-dropping announcement that has stunned cricket fans across the globe, former Indian captain MS Dhoni revealed today that he will be making a full-fledged return to international cricket, including both T20 and Test formats.

“My passion for representing India never went away,” said Dhoni during a surprise press conference in Chennai. “The body feels good, the spirit is stronger than ever, and I'm ready to contribute to Indian cricket once again.”

The BCCI is expected to hold an emergency selection committee meeting to discuss Dhoni’s inclusion in the upcoming bilateral series against South Africa.

Cricket legends including Sachin Tendulkar and Virat Kohli have publicly expressed their excitement, with Kohli tweeting, “Can’t wait to see Thala back in blue. Goosebumps!”
